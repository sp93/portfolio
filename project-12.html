NEWSPAPER AR EXPERIENCE

This project was aimed at adding an AR experience to print media. This is project was made with Vuforia and Unity3D. The video in the link was shot using a Pixel 2 phone.


Goal: To provide extra content relating to articles in a newspaper through a phone using AR.

The process

  • Research
    • Where would users need AR content?
    • What kind of AR content would users need?
    • Where can digital content be augmented considering where/how users read the newspaper?
    • Optimal quality/conditions of image markers required?

  • Execution
    • Optimal image marker testing based on newspaper image area, contrast, colours, detail...etc.
    • Placing and testing the AR content
    • Exporting to APK and testing
