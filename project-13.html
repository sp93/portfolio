KALAAKAAR MAKERSPACE AR TOUR

This project was aimed at making an AR tour for Kalaakaar Makerspace in Pune which shows specifications of equipment present in the makerspace. This is project was made with Vuforia and Unity3D. The video in the link was shot using a Pixel 2 phone.


Goal: To provide a technical-digital tour of a makerspace using AR which gives users, access to general information about the equipment.

The process

  • Research
    • What information do users seek about any workshop equipment?
    • How can information be presented to users in a simple way?
    • How and where can digital content be augmented with respect to user's intuition?
    • How to place AR content in a sensible way?
    • How will users point their devices towards the markers?
    • How will users orient their devices towards the markers?
    • Marker type study, feasibility study and testing.
      • Image based
      • 3D object based

  • Execution
    • Using 3d models produced by photogrammetry as a reference for placing AR content.
    • Optimal image marker testing based on image area, contrast, colours, detail...etc.
    • Testing image marker orientaion and placement such that they don't lead to any obstruction in the equipment's usage.
    • Placing and testing the AR content
    • Exporting to APK and testing
